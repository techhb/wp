Month 1 Goals

    Progressive Overload Summary

    Month 1 3 sets per exercise, moderate weight, focus on learning proper form. Total volume: ~90 sets per week.
    Month 2 4 sets per exercise, increased weight, full range of motion on Ab Carver. Total volume: ~100 sets per week.
    Month 3 4-5 sets per exercise, heavier weight, lower rep ranges on compounds. Total volume: ~110 sets per week.

    Week 13: Deload Week

    After completing Month 3, take a deload week to prevent burnout and injury:

    • Perform the same exercises as Month 3
    • Use only 50% of your normal working weight
    • Do 2 sets instead of 3-4 sets per exercise
    • Focus entirely on form and recovery
    • Then reassess and plan your next 3 months!

    Weekly Tracking Checklist

    • Monday morning weigh-in (after bathroom, before eating)
    • Waist measurement at belly button level
    • Log all workouts with weights used

    Monthly Tracking Checklist

    • Progress photos (front, side, back - same lighting and location)
    • Full body measurements (chest, shoulders, arms, waist, thighs)
    • Review and adjust calories if needed
